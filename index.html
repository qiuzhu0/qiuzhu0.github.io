ChessDB Mobile

Mobile interface for accessing chess opening position evaluations from chessdb.cn's API. Displays statistics from lichess.org players' database and masters' database.

EOcross Solver

Finds solutions of all axes for EOcross, the first step of (the modern interpretation of) the ZZ method to solving the Rubik's Cube. Uses a novel approach of producing solutions in a two-phase process of EO>cross, generating more human-friendly solutions.
